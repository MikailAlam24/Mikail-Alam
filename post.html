POST

POST Houston is a transformative cultural and lifestyle destination located in the heart of downtown Houston. This innovative venue, housed in the former Barbara Jordan Post Office, is a groundbreaking development that redefines urban space. POST Houston features a unique blend of retail, dining, and entertainment options. The complex includes an expansive food hall with culinary delights from around the world, cutting-edge retail spaces, and creative workspaces that foster collaboration and innovation. A highlight of POST Houston is its rooftop park and farm, offering stunning city views and a green retreat above the urban hustle.
